FAQ
Frequently asked questions
Do I need to be home during the detail? +
Nope! As long as we have access to your vehicle and a nearby water/power source, you don't need to be present. We'll notify you when we're done.
Do you bring your own supplies? +
Yes — we bring everything. Professional-grade cleaning products, vacuums, steam cleaners, and microfiber towels. Just provide the car.
How long does a detail take? +
Interior details: 2–3 hours. Exterior details: 1.5–2 hours. Full bundle: 3–5 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.
What's the Extra Dirty Fee? +
If your vehicle's interior has excessive dirt, heavy staining, or pet hair buildup requiring extra time and product, this fee covers that additional labor. We'll always let you know before applying it.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve Baltimore City & County, Silver Spring / Montgomery County, and Washington DC. Travel over 25 miles incurs a +$15 travel fee.
What is your deposit and cancellation policy? +
A 50% deposit is required at booking to secure your appointment. The remaining balance is due upon completion. Cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations under 48 hours forfeit the deposit. Rescheduling is free up to 24 hours before your appointment. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.
How do I pay? +
We accept Cash, Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, and Card via Square. Payment is due upon completion.
